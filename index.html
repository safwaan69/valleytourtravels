Welcome to Kashmir

Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Kashmir is often referred to as "Paradise on Earth" for its breathtaking landscapes, pristine lakes, and snow-capped mountains. This enchanting valley offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality.

Must-Visit Lakes in Kashmir

Dal Lake

Famous for houseboats and shikara rides

Wular Lake

One of Asia’s largest freshwater lakes (30–86 sq. km, fluctuating seasonally), Wular is a Ramsar Wetland of International Importance (designated 1990). Fed by the Jhelum River, it’s vital for flood control and biodiversity.

Manasbal Lake

Known as the “Supreme Gem of Kashmir Lakes,” Manasbal is celebrated for its crystal-clear waters and serene ambiance

Tarsar Lake

A crescent-shaped alpine lake (3,200 m) in the Aru Valley, part of the Tarsar-Marsar trek, known for its reflective waters and lush meadows.
